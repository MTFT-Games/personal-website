Quick guide

This tool is a easy and public view into my todo lists and projects. Friends can use it to take a look at how busy I am or get an idea of what I'm up to, interested employers can take a look into my current projects, or my parents could even use it to see how I'm keeping up with school work.

Clicking on a section or folder in the left navigation pane will fill this area with all the tasks in that section, grouped and sorted according to the settings above.

Clicking the dropdown arrow next to any section or folder will show or hide the folders within so that you can have a less cluttered view of whatever you may be interested in.
