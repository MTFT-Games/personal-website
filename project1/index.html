Who am I?

Hi, I'm Noah Emke. I'm a game developer currenlty attending college at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). I love tinkering with just about everything and figuring out how things work as well as making things out of the concepts that I learn. I am currently seeking a game development or software engineering co-op over the May-August 2022 summer.

What do I work with?

Languages: C#, C++, Java, HTML, CSS
Frameworks: MonoGame, SFML, Box2D
Tools: Unity, Visual Studio, Maya, Git, Gimp
